About Me

I was born in Duluth, Minnesota, on the head of Lake Superior, and have spent much of my life there. I am currently a full-stack web developer based in Denver. I have experience working in politics, chemistry, and aquatic toxicology. My academic background is in history, biochemistry, and mathematics.

My technologies include Node, Express, React, Redux, and MongoDB. I also have experience building mobile applications with React Native.

My hobbies include being ouside hiking, biking, and running. I also thoroughly enjoy music, both consuming it and creating it. My preferred instrument for creation is piano, and I will occasionally pick up a ukulele. With the rest of my time, I enjoy geeking out with machine learning and math.
